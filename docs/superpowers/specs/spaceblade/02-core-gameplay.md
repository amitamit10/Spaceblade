# Core Gameplay

The player auto-runs through the arena from a forward lane on the left side of the screen. Threats spawn ahead and advance toward the player. There is no manual movement; the single-key timing challenge is deciding whether to slash, shoot, dodge, or parry before contact.

One-key controls:

- Tap: sword slash.
- Hold: charge an energy shot.
- Release after charge: fire the energy shot down the threat lane.
- Double tap: dodge.
- Perfect timing press: parry.

Combat identity:

- Player auto-faces the nearest active threat ahead of the runner.
- Sword slash is the default close-range punish.
- Energy shot is the long-range answer and can clear threats before they reach contact range.
- Dodge provides brief invulnerability and spacing.
- Parry rewards precise timing with safety, stun, and score bonus.

Health and failure:

- Player starts each run with 3 hearts.
- Player returns to full HP at the start of each wave.
- Failed defense against enemy contact or attack removes one heart.
- Run ends at zero hearts.

Score and mastery:

- Score increases through kills, parries, clean wave clears, and parry streak bonuses.
- Combo increases on kills and parries.
- Combo resets when the player takes damage.
- Grades are based on final score.
