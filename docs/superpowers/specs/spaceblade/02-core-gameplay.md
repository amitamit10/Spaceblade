# Core Gameplay

The player stands near the center of the arena while enemies rush from left and right spawn zones. Player X-axis movement is visual only; the player remains center-anchored for gameplay.

One-key controls:

- Tap: quick slash.
- Hold: charge.
- Release after charge: heavy slash / shockwave.
- Double tap: dodge.
- Perfect timing press: parry.

Combat identity:

- Player auto-faces the nearest active threat.
- Quick slash is the default close-range punish.
- Heavy slash has broader reach and breaks shields.
- Dodge provides brief invulnerability and spacing.
- Parry rewards precise timing with safety, stun, and score bonus.

Health and failure:

- Player starts each run with 3 hearts.
- Player returns to full HP at the start of each wave.
- Failed defense against enemy contact or attack removes one heart.
- Run ends at zero hearts.

Score and mastery:

- Score increases through kills, parries, clean wave clears, and parry streak bonuses.
- Combo increases on kills and parries.
- Combo resets when the player takes damage.
- Grades are based on final score.
