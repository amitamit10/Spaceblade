# Enemies And Run Structure

Enemy roster:

- `Grunt`: weak melee frontliner, dies in one slash.
- `Runner`: fast striker, crouches then dash stabs through the player.
- `Shield`: blocks frontal quick slashes, countered by heavy slash or parry.
- `Tank`: slow bruiser with 2 HP and a wide heavy swing.
- `Glitch`: late-game teleporting attacker, flickers before reappearing.
- `Boss`: wave 15 final encounter.

Run structure:

- V1 is a 15-wave structured run, not true endless mode.
- Level ID is `Neon-Sector 04`.
- Boss appears on wave 15.
- Difficulty ramps from easy to normal to hard to very hard to insane to boss.
- Normal active threat weight cap is 6.
- Tank and boss each count as threat weight 2.
- No more than 2 tanks may be active.

Arena structure:

- 20 percent left spawn zone.
- 60 percent central combat zone.
- 20 percent right spawn zone.
- Floor platform with three background layers: foreground tech, city mid, distant skyline.

Spawn pacing:

- Waves 1-3 are slower onboarding.
- Waves 4-6 introduce mixed pressure.
- Waves 7-12 add heavier combinations.
- Waves 13-14 are late-game pressure.
- Wave 15 is the boss finish.
